## Suggested detail level

- Under 15 minutes: roughly 1,500–3,000 Chinese characters.
- 15–45 minutes: roughly 3,000–6,000 Chinese characters.
- 45–90 minutes: roughly 5,000–9,000 Chinese characters.
- Over 90 minutes: write by chapter without a mechanical upper limit.

Treat these as completeness guides, not quotas. Remove repetition while preserving distinct ideas, examples, and caveats.
